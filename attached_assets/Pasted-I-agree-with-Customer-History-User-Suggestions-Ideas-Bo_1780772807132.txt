## Pricing

I would not launch at £499.

You've moved beyond that.

My recommendation:

### Founder Beta

**£999 Lifetime**
or

**£120/month for 12 months**
(then owned outright)

Why?

A typical shop owner values their time at far more than £120/month.

If this saves:

* 2 hours per week
* at £50/hour burdened cost

that's ~£400/month of value.

The danger of £499 is not revenue.

The danger is positioning.

People subconsciously think:

> "How good can it be if it's only £499?"

For B2B industrial software, being too cheap can hurt trust.

I would rather sell:

* 100 shops at £999

than

* 200 shops at £499

because the first group will generally be more engaged.
