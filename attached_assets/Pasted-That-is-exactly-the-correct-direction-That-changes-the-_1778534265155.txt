That is exactly the correct direction.

That changes the app from:

> “quote calculator”

into:

> “working estimator workspace.”

That is a major UX improvement.

And honestly, this is probably how the product should fundamentally work.

Because real estimators constantly:

* look at drawing,
* estimate,
* check tolerances,
* go back to drawing,
* check revisions,

* switching tabs,
* zooming,

A side-by-side estimating workspace is much more professional.

This is very similar to:

* CAD workspace logic,
* CAM workspace logic,
* professional estimating workflow.

Very strong idea.
