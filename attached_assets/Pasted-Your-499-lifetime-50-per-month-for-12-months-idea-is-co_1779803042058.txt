Your £499 lifetime / £50 per month for 12 months idea is commercially sensible for beta. It lowers the buying friction and still positions it as owned software rather than a permanent subscription.

**Beta Founder Licence**
£499 one-off
or

Do not make this permanent. Make it clear it is early-access pricing.
